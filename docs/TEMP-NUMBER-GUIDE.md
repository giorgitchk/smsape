# Temporary Phone Number Guide (2026)

A **temporary phone number** (also called a **temp number** or **disposable number**) is a virtual number you rent for a short time to receive SMS verification codes online — without using your real phone.

## When to use a temp number

- Register on apps without exposing your personal number
- Verify accounts on Telegram, WhatsApp, Google, Facebook, etc.
- Test SMS flows in development
- Access services that require a number from another country

## How SmsApe temp numbers work

1. Choose **service** (e.g. Telegram) and **country** (e.g. Germany)
2. Purchase a one-time activation (~20 minutes)
3. Enter the number on the target platform
4. OTP arrives in your [SmsApe dashboard](https://smsape.com/cabinet/sms)
5. No SMS? Balance is **automatically refunded**
